Study Buddy Finder

The # 1 way to find other students in your classes to study with

Getting Started:

  1. Use the search bar at the top of the page to search for a specific user or search for a class to find all users who currently have that class added to their profile.
  2. Click on a user to see their profile where you can add them as a friend or chat with them.
  3. Use the Chat feature to message other users on your friends list.
  4. Add classes to your profile through the Course List tab or from your profile page by clicking the "Add Class" button.
  5. Manage your classes and friends from the profile page.
  6. Set meetings with your friends and view upcoming meetings on the Calendar tab.
